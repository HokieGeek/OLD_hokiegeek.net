Using the Quick Viewer

The Quick Viewer enables you to view the images, cine clips, reports, and voice clips associated with patient studies on any personal computer that has access to a Web browser. The Quick Viewer is accessed from the MI Disc View compact disc (CD). The CD can contain images and/or patient documentation belonging to one or multiple patient studies.
