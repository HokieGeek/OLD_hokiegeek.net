Displaying the Study list

The Study list is displayed if the MI Disc View CD contains multiple studies and/or studies for multiple patients.

The Study list is displayed in one of the following ways:

  • Automatically, after your insert the CD into the CD-ROM drive. For details, see Starting the Quick Viewer.
  • Manually, from an open study.

To display the Study list from an open study:

  • In an open study, click the Study list icon on the main toolbar.

    • The Study list is displayed.
