Displaying series

For studies that contain multiple series, thumbnails are displayed in the Thumbnail Preview area on the Images page. Each thumbnail represents a series. You can select the series you want to display by selecting the thumbnail.

To select a series to display:

  1. Click the Images tab to display the Images page, if it is not already displayed.

  2. In the Thumbnail Preview area, click the thumbnail that represents the series you want to view.

    3. The series is displayed in the viewport.
