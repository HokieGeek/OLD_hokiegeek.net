Starting the Quick Viewer

The Quick Viewer starts automatically when you insert the MI Disc View CD into the DR-ROM drive.

To start the Quick Viewer:

  • Insert the MI Disc View CD into the CD-ROM drive:

If...

Then...

CD contains multiple studies and/or studies for multiple patients

The Study list is displayed.

  • Click a study in the list to open it.

CD contains a single study

The study is displayed on the study pages.

The MI Disc View study pages are displayed according to the items the study contains. For further details, see Displaying the Study list and Opening studies.
