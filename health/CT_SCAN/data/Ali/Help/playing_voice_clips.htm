Playing voice clips

The voice clips in an open study are listed on the Documents page. Voice clips are stored on the MI Disc View CD as WAV files. You can play a voice clip, using any of the following media players:

  • Apple QuickTime™ Player

    • Note: To play WAV files using the Apple QuickTime™ Player, you need to install the Apple QuickTime™ Pro audio and video authoring software. For details, visit the web site at: www.apple.com
  • Windows Media® Player

For details on obtaining a media player, see Cine Clips page.

To play a voice clip:

  1. Click the Documents tab to display the Documents page.

  2. In the Documents sidebar, click the link to the voice clip you want to play.

  3. Click Play Voice Clip.

    4. The voice clip is played in the appropriate media player.

  4. To close the voice clip, close the media player.
