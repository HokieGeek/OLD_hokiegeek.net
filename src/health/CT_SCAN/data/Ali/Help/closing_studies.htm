Closing studies

The Quick Viewer enables you to view one study at a time. When you open another study, the study that was open previously is closed automatically. To open another study, you need to select it from the Study list. See also, Displaying the Study list.

To close a study in the Quick Viewer:

  • In an open study, do one of the following:

If...

Then...

MI Disc View CD contains a single study

Close the Web browser.

Follow the procedure described in Exiting the Quick Viewer.

MI Disc View CD contains one or more studies

Click the Study list icon on the main toolbar.

The study is closed and the Study list is displayed. To open another study, see Opening studies.
