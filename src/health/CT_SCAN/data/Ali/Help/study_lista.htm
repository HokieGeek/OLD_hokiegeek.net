Study list

The Study list provides a list of the patient studies that are stored on the MI Disc View CD. The Study list displays information about the studies, including patient name, study date, procedure type, and modality.

Note: The patient name is provided only if studies of multiple patients are stored on the CD.

The Study list is displayed only if the MI Disc View CD contains multiple studies for one or more patients.

The Study list is displayed automatically on most computers when you insert the MI Disc View CD into the CD-ROM drive. You can choose which study to display by selecting it from the Study list. However, if the MI Disc View CD contains a single study, the study is displayed instead.
