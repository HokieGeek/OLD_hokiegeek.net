Viewing images in a series

The Series Navigation bar enables you to move through the series that is currently displayed in the viewport. You can:

  • View single images
  • Jump to a specific image in the series
  • Scroll through the entire series

To view images of a series:

  1. Display the series you want to view, if it is not already displayed.
  2. Use the tools on the Series Navigation bar to move through the images, as follows:

If...

Then...

You want to move through the series, viewing one image at a time

Do any of the following:

  • Click the Next arrow to view the next image

  • Click the Previous arrow to view the previous image

You want to jump to a specific image in the series

Click the chit that represents the image of interest.

You want to scroll through a series, viewing images in short succession

Click the chit that represents the first image you want to view, and then drag the mouse pointer across the bar.

The images are displayed in the viewport, as specified.
